Isabella Celeste Maund

London-based Artist and Event Organiser

HomeWorkEventsContact

During her time at Goldsmiths, Isabella has been actively involved in organising various exhibitions and has been a core member of Hacksmiths, the student run Tech Society. Previously as the Diversity and Inclusion Officer, and now as the Secretary of Hacksmiths, she runs Lovelace, a series of events for women and non binary people in/interested in tech at Goldsmiths. These events have been sponsored by the likes of Spotify and are supported by the Department of Computing.
